About Me

I am a tenured assistant professor (Maître de Conférences) of Laboratory in Mathematics and Computer Science (MICS) at CentraleSupélec and a researcher in the "Fédération de Mathématiques de CentraleSupélec" (CNRS FR3487). Before joining CentraleSupélec MICS, I was postdoc respectively in Department of Mathematics, University of Michigan (2018-2020) and Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford (2016-2018). I received the Ph.D degree in Oct, 2016, from Centre de Mathématiques Appliquées, Ecole Polytechnique.
