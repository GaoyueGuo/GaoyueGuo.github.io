ABOUT ME

I am a tenured assistant professor (Maître de Conférences) of Laboratory in Mathematics and Computer Science (MICS) at CentraleSupélec, Université Paris-Saclay. Before joining CentraleSupélec MICS, I was postdoc respectively in Department of Mathematics, University of Michigan (2018-2020) and Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford (2016-2018). I received the Ph.D degree in Oct, 2016, from Département de Mathématiques Appliquées, Ecole Polytechnique. My thesis advisor is Professor Nizar Touzi. Prior to that, I obtained an engineer diploma (Diplôme d'ingénieur) from Ecole Polytechnique.

My Research interests

  • Point processes
  • Anomaly detection
  • Robustness
  • Data depth
  • Neuroscience
