About me

I am a Asssitant Professor at totototParietal team at INRIA Paris-Saclay, since may 2022, working with Thomas Moreau and Alexandre Gramfort. I work on point processes and their application to model stimuli induced patterns in M/EEG signals. I organize the weekly seminar of the MIND (known formerly as Parietal) team at INRIA, feel free to send me an email if you would like to present us your work!

Prior to this position, I did my PhD at Télécom Paris (Institut Polytechnique de Paris) in the Team Signal, Statistique et Apprentissage (S2A). My advisors were Pavlo Mozharovskyi, Stéphan Clémençon and Florence d'Alché-Buc. My PhD focused on anomaly detection with a special focus on time-series/functional data as well as robust estimation applied to a wide variety of ML problems such as probability metrics estimation. Here is the manuscript.
